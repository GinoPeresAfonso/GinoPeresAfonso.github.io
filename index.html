Multidisciplinary

What I Do

I design experiences that help people think differently, work through uncertainty, and turn ideas into something tangible. With a background in ideation design and motion picture (VFX), my work sits at the intersection of facilitation, storytelling, and making by translating complex ideas into practical experiences, prototypes, and outputs. I focus on creating environments where people learn by doing, testing, and failing forward.
